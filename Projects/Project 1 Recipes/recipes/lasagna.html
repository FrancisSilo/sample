Lasagna

Description

Description of the Lasagna recipe...

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet, then mix with tomato sauce.
  3. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake in the oven until bubbly and golden brown.
  6. Let it rest before serving for the flavors to meld together.
