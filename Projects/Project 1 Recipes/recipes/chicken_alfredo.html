Chicken Alfredo

Description

Description of the Chicken Alfredo recipe...

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook the chicken breast until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Add butter and minced garlic to the skillet, then pour in the heavy cream.
  4. Simmer the sauce until it thickens, then stir in grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the creamy Alfredo sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan and fresh parsley.
