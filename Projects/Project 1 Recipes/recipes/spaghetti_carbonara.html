Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Description of the Spaghetti Carbonara recipe...

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Guanciale or pancetta
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  3. Cook the guanciale or pancetta until crispy, then add the cooked spaghetti to the pan.
  4. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan and black pepper.
